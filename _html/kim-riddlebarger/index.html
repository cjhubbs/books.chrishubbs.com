Books by Kim Riddlebarger

Man of Sin

by Kim Riddlebarger · published 2006 · read 2008-11-21
★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
A companion book to his The Case for Amillenialism, which I read earlier this year, in this book Riddlebarger examines the different perspectives on the Antichrist and argues for a traditional Reformed Presbyterian view. Nothing earthshattering, but good stuff.

A Case for Amillennialism: Understanding the End Times

by Kim Riddlebarger · published 2003 · read 2008-07-05
★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
A spirited defense of amillennialism, with arguments for why the other common positions are incorrect. Quite readable, seemingly fair. Leaves me with much to ponder.
