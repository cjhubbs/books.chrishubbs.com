Books by Cormac McCarthy

The Road

by Cormac McCarthy · published 2006 · read 2007-10-28
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
No, I didn’t read this one because Oprah recommended it. But I’d heard talk of McCarthy and realized I’d never read anything of his, so I grabbed this one. Wowza. Dark, harrowing, but not entirely despairing. A chilling picture of the post-apocalyptic experience.

All the Pretty Horses

by Cormac McCarthy · published 1993 · read 2007-11-06
★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
OK, so I didn’t really plan to read two McCarthy novels in a row, but I ditched the Salman Rushdie book about a hundred pages in. So instead I catch up on McCarthy. Superlatives fail me. Not a complex plot, but beautifully written and picturesque in a way you rarely find. I can understand why they rate McCarthy as one of the top American authors. Good stuff.

The Passenger

by Cormac McCarthy · published 2022 · read 2023-02-07
★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
On one hand, it feels like if I'm not rating a McCarthy book highly, it's a shortcoming on my part, not his. And yet it doens't feel like top-tier McCarthy. Still distinctly his voice, but not quite in the tier of his classics.
