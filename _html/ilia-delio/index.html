Birth of a Dancing Star

by Ilia Delio · published 2019 · read 2023-08-04
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
As a review on Goodreads said, it's a little more a recount than a memoir with an overarching theme and narrative. But it's a great companion piece to Delio's theological books; it provides a vivid perspective of the woman behind the ideas and words.

Christ in Evolution

by Ilia Delio · published 2008 · read 2023-07-22
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Delio says that Christianity's cosmology needs to keep up with science, and explores through the writing of Teilhard de Chardin, Thomas Merton, Raimon Panikkar, and others just what that might look like. Mind bending in all the best ways.
